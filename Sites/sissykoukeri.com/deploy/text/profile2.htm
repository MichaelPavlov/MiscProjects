Sissy Koukeri has been designing and creating unique, handmade Jewelry for over 15 years. Her specially handcrafted designs are inspired from all kinds of things. Trading spaces: bank branches, shop networks, Institutional spaces: welcome and reception spaces, adding value to companies (headquarters, company visits, showrooms), Working spaces: offices and professional environments. To confront the new commercial challenges faced by brands and meet the most complex problems faced by its clients, the company designers have adopted an original and innovative approach.
